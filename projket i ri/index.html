Today, I was attacked by a wooden board.

Now, you must be thinking “someone attacked OP with a wooden board”. But no, that’s not how it happened. It was just me and the board, no other individuals being directly involved in the incident. My dad has tasked my brother and I with tearing down our old fence ourselves. It’s a decently sized fence, around 10 feet tall or so. We were unscrewing the screws, piling the wood up, and breaking the thinner boards in half to fit them into the car to give to one of my dad’s friends. We do 1 section every day. Today, we chose not to use the Jigsaw to cut the thinner boards (they’re as thick as a regular plank of wood). Instead, we would prop them up diagonally on the porch and stomp in the middle to break it in half. Now, one of the halves, right after being stomped, catapulted up. Think of it as when a cartoon character steps on a rake and the handle shoots up and hits them in the face. But, instead of hitting me in the face, the wooden edge nailed me right in the knee. This whole thing happened in about half of a second. I limped inside and curled up on the couch for a good 5 minutes, breathing heavily and waiting for the pain to subside. Luckily, my knee (hopefully) wasn’t broken. Somehow, it wasn’t even bruised (although the pain is still there). I definitely won’t be running or jumping much for the next few days. TLDR: Lazy teenager nearly breaks his knee with a plank of wood.

Introduction

“Keep driving!” Shouted Khalid. Him and his buddy were being chased by a group of bandits who were not so happy with their presence in the city. He would lead those who chased after him a dance and it was as if his enemies were chasing someone non-existent. He drove through the bumpy roads and turned left and right. All the while the bandits were trying to catch up to them and lead them to a dead end or cause them to crash. The steering wheel was never seated in one position. It was gripped by the driver's hands tightly as if he was trying to squeeze something out of it. He was very cautious not to hit any civilians in the way however there were a lot of damages to the car and very nearly did the enemies sometimes jump on to Khalids vehicle, but Khalid and his buddy held them off and eventually got away. I told you we could do it,” said Khalids buddy. “I never doubted you one bit,” replied Khalid whilst breathing heavily from the fear of making that jump. “That was... crazy,” said Khalid whilst gripping his hands on his knees and trying to catch his breath. The right side of his face was covered with mud and he had a few bruises on his arm and some very worn-out clothes from all the travelling and walking and running he had to endure in order to make sure he completes his journey and finds the treasure he has been so eagerly seeking. “Look,” said Khalids buddy as he tapped him on his arm. “We’re here.” Khalid immediately stood up straight and stopped and stared at the sight which brought pleasure to his eyes. He finally believed he was going to get the answer he was so desperately seeking for.

Chapter 1: How It All Began

From a young age Khalid was absorbed into reading books about how famous explorers found new parts of the world. Or stories which told of hidden treasures which still to this day, no one has been able to find He also read about how archaeologists dug up bones or ancient sites buried underground to find out more about the people of the past. This was all interesting for Khalid and he was obviously fascinated by all of these stories and discoveries. But there was one story which Khalid was very curious about. It was a treasure which would lead to a lost kingdom. Khalid read about this treasure day and night and he would dream that he would be the first to find this treasure and no one would beat him to it. It was called... ‘The Black Stone.’ Although there are many speculations on the back stone truly existing or not. Khalid would spend his free time reading about the Black Stone because he truly believed that ancient treasure wasn't a fairy tale but was true. He believed that the only reason why the treasure hunters of the past believed it to be false was because they themselves failed to find it. Khalid had many reasons why this was. It could be because they were missing some information on its exact location. Like a map or more information about its history and where it ended up. It could be that the Black Stone was passed down by generation after generation and now someone needs to contact the family which possess the Black Stone. Or at least someone connected to the family. Khalid researched day and night as a child, as a teenager and now even as a grown man. However, during his childhood his father would admonish him for reading and researching about something which experts doubt even exists. What does a small child like Khalid hope to gain? He has not even explored his own city properly let alone exploring the world for treasure. But Khalid was confident and would not let anyone get in his way. From a child he would say, “I will find the Black Stone and when I do, you will all realise that It wasn’t me that was mistaken, rather it was the explorers of the past.” Still his family laughed at him but allowed him to read because the child's imagination will soon fade away and he will come to realize what life is truly about. Although Khalid loved reading these treasure hunting stories and dreamed about them in his sleep, Khalid was a Muslim and a practicing one at that. He loved Islam and would always be reminded to hold firm on to it and not let anyone or anything stop him from being firm upon it. Although his family doubted that he would find the Black Stone or any treasure at all, they did not doubt that he would stop holding on to Islam.
